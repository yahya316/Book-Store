The Starless Sea

Erin Morgenstern

$14.99

In Stock

Zachary Ezra Rawlins is a graduate student in Vermont when he discovers a mysterious book hidden in the stacks. As he turns the pages, he’s drawn into a subterranean world filled with secret libraries, hidden stories, and magical portals. The Starless Sea is a mesmerizing tale of love, loss, and the power of stories, weaving together myth, folklore, and adventure in a way that captivates the imagination.
